Bethany Hamilton

Bethany Hamilton was born in Lihue Kauai on February 8, 1990 to a family of surfers who had her on a board from a young age. After being attacked by a tiger shark and losing her arm, Bethany went on to become an accomplished surfer, winning national championships, writing a book which later was adapted into a movie, and advocating and inspiring those who have also lost or were born without apendages like arms or legs.
